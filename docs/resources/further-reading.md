## Research & Studies

### Key Studies to Know

**Mold Illness:**

- Shoemaker RC, House DE. ["Sick building syndrome (SBS) and exposure to water-damaged buildings"](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/16697916/) *Neurotoxicol Teratol* (2006)
- Brewer JH et al. ["Detection of mycotoxins in patients with chronic fatigue syndrome"](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3705282/) *Toxins* (2013)

**Omega-3 and Inflammation:**

- Calder PC. ["Omega-3 fatty acids and inflammatory processes"](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3257651/) *Nutrients* (2010)
- Goldberg RJ, Katz J. ["Omega-3 fatty acid supplementation for inflammatory joint pain"](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/17335973/) *Pain* (2007)

**Curcumin:**

- Hewlings SJ, Kalman DS. ["Curcumin: A Review of Its Effects on Human Health"](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5664031/) *Foods* (2017)
- Kuptniratsaikul V et al. ["Curcumin compared with ibuprofen in knee osteoarthritis"](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3964021/) *Clin Interv Aging* (2014)

**Medicinal Mushrooms:**

- Wasser SP. ["Medicinal mushroom science: Current perspectives"](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/25271984/) *Int J Med Mushrooms* (2014)
- Phan CW et al. ["Therapeutic potential of culinary-medicinal mushrooms"](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/26559695/) *J Ethnopharmacol* (2015)
- Mori K et al. ["Improving effects of Hericium erinaceus on cognitive function"](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/18844328/) *Phytother Res* (2009)
